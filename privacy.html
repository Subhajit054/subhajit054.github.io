Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

QR Maker (accessible at subhajit054.github.io) is a free, browser-based tool for generating QR codes. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

QR Maker does not collect, store, or transmit any of the text, links, or data you enter to generate QR codes. All QR code processing happens locally on your device using client-side JavaScript.

We do not create user accounts, store personal information, or track individual usage.

2. Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites hosted on GitHub Pages, standard server logs may temporarily record:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Referring pages
  • Date and time of access

These logs are controlled by GitHub, not QR Maker, and are not used to identify individual users.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect non-personal, aggregated statistics such as:

  • Page views
  • Device categories
  • Anonymous usage trends

This information helps us improve the site. You may opt out by using browser privacy features or tools like Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

4. Advertising and Cookies (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements.

Google and third-party vendors may use cookies, including the DoubleClick (DART) cookie, to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history.

Your Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • You can disable ad personalization entirely for your browser using NAI consumer opt-out tools.
  • You may also block cookies in your browser settings.

For more details on how Google uses data, visit: Google Ads Privacy & Terms .

5. Third-Party Links

QR Maker may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. We encourage users to review their respective privacy policies.

6. GDPR / CCPA Notice (General Statement)

While QR Maker does not collect personal data directly, third-party services like Google Analytics and Google AdSense may process information according to their own policies.

Users in regions such as the EU and California may:

  • Request data access or deletion from Google services
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Use browser privacy controls to limit data processing

7. Children's Privacy

QR Maker is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
