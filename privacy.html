Privacy Policy

Last updated: Nov 30, 2025

QR Maker (hosted at subhajit054.github.io) is a free tool to generate QR codes in your browser. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

All QR code generation happens locally in your web browser. We do not collect or store QR content (URLs, text, WiFi credentials) on our servers. The site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to collect aggregated usage data (page views, device type, country). This helps us improve features and performance.

Cookies and tracking

We use Google Analytics to measure site traffic. Analytics collects non-identifying data such as pages visited and device info. You can opt out of analytics using browser controls or ad blockers. If you would like to opt out of analytics collection for this site, please use your browser's tracking protection settings.

Ads

We use Google AdSense to display ads. AdSense may use cookies and device identifiers to serve relevant ads. For details on how Google collects and uses information, visit the Google Ads policies. You can control ad personalization via your Google account settings.

Third-party links

This site may include links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. Please review their policies before sharing personal information.
