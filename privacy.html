Privacy Policy

Last updated: Nov 30, 2025

QR Maker (hosted at subhajit054.github.io) is a client-side web tool for generating QR codes. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

QR creation is performed in the user's browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the data encoded in your QR codes. The only server-side data used is standard web server logs for GitHub Pages (not controlled by us) and site analytics for usage statistics.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect aggregated, non-identifying usage statistics (pageviews, device categories, countries). This helps improve the site. You can opt out or block analytics via browser settings or extensions.

Ads and cookies

Ads on this site are provided by Google AdSense. AdSense may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads. For details and opt-out options visit your Google account ad settings.

Third-party links

This site may link to third-party pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites. Review their privacy policies if you follow external links.
