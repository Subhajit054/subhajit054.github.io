QR Maker — Free Online QR Code Generator

QR Maker is a privacy-first web tool that lets you create QR codes instantly in your browser. No uploading, no accounts — everything runs client-side so your data never leaves your device.

Open QR Maker — Generate Now

What you can do

Use QR Maker to generate QR codes for:

  • Web URLs (https://...), short links and landing pages
  • WiFi credentials (SSID/WPA/password) — share with guests
  • Phone numbers, SMS messages, email templates and plain text
  • Download QR as PNG or scalable SVG for printing

Why this tool is safe

All QR generation is performed in your browser using JavaScript. We do not log the text or credentials that you encode. The site uses Google Analytics (GA4) for anonymous traffic reports and AdSense to display ads. For full details see our Privacy Policy.

Tips for best results

  1. Use https:// links for produced QR codes to avoid browser warnings.
  2. When printing, choose SVG or a high DPI PNG (600–1200 px).
  3. For WiFi QR, set the correct encryption type (WPA/WPA2) and test the QR on multiple devices.

If you need more help, check our guides: How to create QR and WiFi QR guide.
