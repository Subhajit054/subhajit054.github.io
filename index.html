Welcome — Try QR Maker

Create QR codes for URLs, text, phone numbers, email, or WiFi credentials. QR Maker is a small, privacy-first web tool that generates high-quality QR codes entirely in your browser—no signup or server required.

Why use this tool?

  • Fast, client-side QR generation — works offline after load
  • No tracking of the text you enter — generation happens locally
  • Download PNG (and if available, SVG) at multiple sizes
  • Supports many QR types: URL, text, email, phone, WiFi

How it works

When you enter content and click generate, the page builds the QR code using a small JavaScript library and renders it to your screen. You can then download the image. Nothing you type is sent to any server.

Quick usage guide

  1. Open the QR Maker tool.
  2. Paste a URL or type text.
  3. Choose size (256 or 512 recommended for printing).
  4. Click Generate and then Download.

Example uses

  • Business cards and resumes
  • Event posters and tickets
  • Restaurant menus and shop promotions
  • WiFi sharing without telling the password

Frequently asked questions

Is my data stored?

No — the QR content is processed locally in your browser and not saved on this server. See Privacy Policy.

Can I use the QR Codes for business?

Yes — generated QR codes are free to use commercially. Test scanners and printing quality before large distribution.
