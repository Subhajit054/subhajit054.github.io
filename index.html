How it helps

QR codes are used everywhere: menus, business cards, product packaging, event tickets and quick sharing. QR Maker focuses on simplicity: pick a type, paste your data, customize color/size, and download. For WiFi sharing use SSID|WPA|password format and guests can connect by scanning the QR.

Privacy & Security

All QR generation happens client-side in your browser; no data is sent to our servers. We never store or sell your data. For more details see the Privacy Policy.

Tips for best results

  • Use https:// links for website QR codes.
  • Choose high contrast colors (dark foreground, light background).
  • For printing use SVG or a high-size PNG (600–1200 px).
  • Test the QR on multiple phones before printing.
