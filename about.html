About

Hi — I am Subhajit Sen, a B.Tech student and hobby web developer. I build small web tools to help students and small businesses. QR Maker started as a simple student project and became a small, useful tool to create QR codes quickly without installing anything.

Why I built this

I wanted an open, privacy-friendly QR generator that runs in the browser and keeps user data local. It is designed to be fast, free and easy to use.
