About QR Maker

Hi — I’m Subhajit Sen, a B.Tech student and web developer from India. I build small web tools and projects that help students, creators and small businesses solve everyday problems. QR Maker started as a simple student project and became a lightweight, privacy-first utility for creating reliable QR codes without any server-side processing.

Quick facts
Purpose
Generate high-quality QR codes quickly and download them as PNG images.
Privacy
Everything is generated locally in your browser — no text or URLs are stored on the server.
Cost
Free to use — no registration required.

Why I built this

I noticed students and small businesses needing an easy way to create QR codes for events, business cards, posters and simple payment links. Many tools either required sign-up, tracked users, or added watermarks. I wanted a fast, clean tool that respects privacy and works on any device — so I made QR Maker.

How QR Maker works

The QR generation runs entirely in your browser using a small JavaScript library. When you enter text or a URL and click “Generate”, the page draws the QR code on a canvas or image and lets you download it as a PNG. Because everything happens locally, nothing is sent to any external server.

Who should use it

QR Maker is useful for:

  • Students creating contact pages or event flyers
  • Small businesses printing QR codes for product info or menus
  • Developers and designers needing quick test QR codes
  • Anyone who wants a simple, no-friction QR generator

Common questions (FAQ)

Is my data stored?

No — QR Maker does not store the text you type. All processing is done in your browser.

Can I create large resolution QR codes for print?

Yes — choose a larger size option on the generator page (256 or 512) and download the PNG. For professional print, export at the highest size available and use vector tools if needed.

Can I use these QR codes commercially?

Yes — generated QR codes are free to use for personal and commercial projects. See the Terms & Conditions for details.
