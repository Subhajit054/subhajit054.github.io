WiFi QR Generator

A WiFi QR allows visitors to scan a code and connect to your network automatically (many phones support this feature). This page explains the format and safety tips.

WiFi QR format

The standard format for WiFi QR content is:

WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;H:false;;

  • T: is the authentication type: WEP, WPA, or left empty for no password.
  • S: is the SSID (network name).
  • P: is the password (leave blank for open networks).
  • H: is true if the SSID is hidden; otherwise false.

Example

For a WPA network named CafeNetwork with password guest1234, the QR data is: 

WIFI:T:WPA;S:CafeNetwork;P:guest1234;H:false;;

How to create

  1. Open QR Maker.
  2. Choose the "Text" or "WiFi" type (if available) and paste the formatted string above.
  3. Click Generate and test with a phone by scanning the result.

Security and privacy tips

  • Share WiFi QR only with trusted guests.
  • Change guest network passwords periodically.
  • For public places, consider a guest network with limited access rather than your main network.

Troubleshooting

  • If devices don't connect, verify the SSID and password are exactly correct (they are case-sensitive).
  • Some devices may not support hidden SSIDs or certain authentication types.
